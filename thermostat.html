Thermostats

Improve your current heating/cooling system with a smart thermostat that can be controlled from anywhere.

Smart thermostats connect to your Wi-Fi network and can be controlled either via the manufacturers app and HomeKit, delivering control to your iPhone, iWatch, iPad. These thermostats can respond as part of an automation, Eg: turn the heating off when nobody is home.  

The result is that they can work proactively to keep your home at the most comfortable temperature for you without you having to lift a finger. And they do it all with a high degree of efficiency, capable of reducing energy consumption by up to 30%.

Smart Thermostats 

There are many different types of smart thermostats. The Nest thermostat is popular (due to its sleek appearance.. but it's also the most expensive at $350 unfitted). Alternatives start from $180. 



WiFi Controllers

Wifi controllers are used when a smart thermostat isn't required.  A number of major brands produce Wifi controllers that can be purchased and installed by heating/cooling specialists.  Depending upon brand these can vary in cost from $250 up to ~$1100. 

Unique Styles

HomeKit renders the thermostat to look like a stylish dial that can be controlled by your finger, or alternatively by voice. "Hey Siri, set thermostat to 30 degrees".
