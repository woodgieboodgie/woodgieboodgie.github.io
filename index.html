House-Mate

One voice enabled app to control your home

Use your mobile device from any location to operate your home's lighting, alarm system, security cameras, blinds, irrigation, heating & cooling, and more... 
This solution suits existing homes or new builds and delivers truly centralised home automation.
You choose whether its via Apple HomeKit™, Google Home™, or Amazon Alexa™.

Lighting

Dim your lights. Turn them on or off. Schedule lights to come on or off at certain times of the day. "Hey Siri, turn off the downstairs lights".

Blinds

Integrate your existing motorised blinds or curtains. Window furnishings can be opened, closed, or programmed to operate according to temperature or time of day. "Siri, close the main room blinds". 

Security

Secure your home. Your alarm system automatically arms itself when the last person leaves the house. Video doorbell and security camera integration. Real time notification of instrusion.

Irrigation & Pool and Spas

Operate your garden watering (up to 8 zones) on demand or via a programmable schedule that will delay watering for 24hrs if it rains.
Control your swimming pool functions such as temperature, pumps, chlorination... all can be automated.

Heating and Cooling

Automatically turn on the heating when your approach home or switch it off when the last person leaves. Easily bring your existing system into the smart home.

Audio / Visual

Control your TV, projector, or amplifier via HomeKit.  Capture regularly used buttons from your remote controls and operate them via your phone or by voice. "Hey Siri, turn on TV, Channel 7"

Garage door

Control garages or gates via HomeKit, query their status easily from anywhere. Compatible with most common brands. Place a camera in the garage and accept deliveries, then close the garage door. "Siri, is the garage door closed?"
