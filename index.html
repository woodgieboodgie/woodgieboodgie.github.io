House-Mate Automation

  Simple and affordable home automation.

Let us show you how centralised control of your smart home can change the way you live.
Your mobile devices can operate your home from anywhere for optimal comfort, security, and peace of mind.



 

One voice enabled app to control your home

Use your mobile device from any location to operate your home's lighting, alarm system, security cameras, blinds, irrigation, heating & cooling, and more... 
This solution suits existing homes or new builds and delivers truly centralised home automation.
You choose whether its via Apple HomeKit™, Google Home™, or Amazon Alexa™.

Lighting

Dim your lights or operate them without being at home. Schedule your lights to turn on or off at certain times of the day for added security, or simply say "Hey Siri, turn off the downstairs lights".


Blinds

Integrate your existing motorised blinds or curtains. Window furnishings can be opened or closed on demand, or programmed to operate according to temperature or time of day. "Siri, close the main room blinds". 

Security

Secure your home. The alarm system automatically arms itself when the last person leaves the house. Answer the doorbell using your phone from anywhere. CCTV security camera integration available. Real time intrusion notification.

Irrigation

Operate your garden watering (up to 8 zones) on demand or via a programmable schedule that will delay watering for 24hrs if its forecasted to rain, or turn off the sprinklers instantly when you see it raining.

Heating and Cooling

Automatically turn on the heating when your approach home or switch it off when the last person leaves. Bring your existing air conditioning or central heating system into the smart home.

Audio / Visual

Control your TV, projector, or amplifier via HomeKit.  Capture regularly used buttons from your remote controls and operate them via your phone or by voice. "Hey Siri, turn on TV"

Garage door

Control garages or gates via HomeKit, query their status easily from anywhere. Compatible with most common brands. "Hey Siri, close the garage door"

Pool and Spa

Control any swimming pool or spa functions such as temperature, pumps, chlorination. Pre-heat the spa before you come home. "Hey Siri, set the spa temperature to 35 degrees"
