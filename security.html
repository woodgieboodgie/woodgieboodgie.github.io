Security System

Consumers have come to expect increasingly high standards and sophistication from home security systems

Have your security system arm automatically when the last person leaves the house and deactivate when the first person arrives home. Setup automations to occur when the alarm is armed , eg: turn off lights, TVs, close blinds. Hard wired solutions suit larger homes and wireless (Zigbee) solutions for smaller dwellings.

Integrate smoke detectors, movement sensors, cameras, door/window sensors. All can send notifications if triggered when the house is armed. Keyless entry compatibility. Numerous locks available to suit different designs.

Unparalled security

Sophisticated and affordable cameras, either wired or wireless that can detect motion and record whats happening. See real time what every camera is seeing. Purpose built hard wired alarm systems are available and integrate should you wish for more than just cameras.

Door and window sensors

Arm the perimeter of your home automatically when you go to bed. Door and window sensors are a very effective way to secure the home and can be wired or run on battery.

From anywhere

Simple operation of the alarm using HomeKit.
Once the house is armed any breach will notify you via iPhone, iPad, iWatch.
