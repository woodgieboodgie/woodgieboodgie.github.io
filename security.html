Security

Consumers have come to expect increasingly high standards and sophistication from home security systems

Have your alarm activate automatically when the last person leaves the house and deactivate when the first person arrives home.

Trigger endless number of scenes when the alarm is armed , eg: turn off lights, TVs. 

Receive notifications on your phone when sensors trigger (iWatch iPhone iPad).  


Using both wired and wireless systems, we can design sophisticated solution to perform indoors and/or outdoors.

Add smoke detectors, movement sensors, door/window sensors. All will send notifications if triggered when the house is armed.

Movement sensors that perform dual functions. They can be utilised to activate lights or scenes, but also act as alarm movement sensors when the house is armed. Now thats smart!

Unparalled security

Sophisticated and affordable cameras, either wired or wireless that can detect motion and record whats happening. See real time what every camera is seeing.

Door and window sensors

Arm the perimeter of your home automatically when you go to bed. Door and window sensors are a very effective way to secure the home and can be powered or run on battery.

Automatically arm and disarm

The system will automatically arm the house when the last person leaves and can be set to launch any scene at the same time.
eg: turn off the heating, or the TV.

From anywhere

Simple operation of the alarm using HomeKit.
Once the house is armed any breach will notify you via iPhone, iPad, iWatch.
