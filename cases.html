Case Studies

References from completed installations

We thoroughly enjoy working with our clients to deliver their vision. House-mate is so scalable that clients like the freedom of choice when it comes to their entry point.  

Start with any part of the system and build it out in a way that suits you.

Large Caulfield Home

The client desired a retro fit that could harness the full power of House-mate. Starting with lighting, the solution progressed to gates, garage door, TV, amplifier, irrigation.
No wall switches were changed, 28 light circuits controllable with complete voice activation delivered. Thermostat was the final piece for this client with integration to large Daikin units procured.

St.Kilda Triple Storey Apartment

Existing CBUS installation where the client wanted control via HomeKit and also desired several additional lighting circuits added.  The client also desired control of blinds (Acmeda), Sony TV, projector, amplifier, and motorised screen.

East Brighton Unit

Client wanted thermostats, a security system, control of 8 light circuits, TV control, and some on-wall power points.
Procured a Nest thermostat to control a Brivis central heating unit, introduced client TV into Homekit. 

Peninsula Mansion

This is a large scale project involves all aspects of the House-mate solution.  We have been working with electricians, irrigation, heating/cooling to ensure all systems are fully compatible before purchase.
