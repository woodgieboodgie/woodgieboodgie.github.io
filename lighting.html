Lighting

Centralised control of every light circuit in your house

The simplest and most scalable lighting automation solution.

When it comes to a smart home, lighting is one of the simplest ways to get going.

Our lighting solution can be retro fitted into any existing home or installed into new homes. Use your existing lights and wiring circuits. A circuit can contain multiple lights.

Dimmable or non-dimmable switches to control your light circuits are available.

Control your lights from HomeKit, create automated scenes, or simply say "Hey Siri, Main room lights 20%". 

Keep your switches 

Many solutions require new switches or wall plates, and that can become expensive.

Our solution can utilise your existing switches.

The smarts are in here

WiFi wall switches must be installed by qualified electricians behind your existing wall plates.
They take up next to no space and can be hidden easily. All meet strict AU design standards.

To dim or not to dim

On/Off switches and Dimmable switches are available. If your existing bulbs and/or transformers are dimmable you can instruct Siri to dim the lights too!

HomeKit

Instruct HomeKit by voice or alternatively use the rich interface presented via the app on your iPhone to control light circuits.  Dimmable circuits can dim to any percentage.
