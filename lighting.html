Lighting

Centralised control of every light circuit in your house

When it comes to a smart home, lighting is one of the simplest ways to get going. There are multiple ways to add intelligence to your lighting; smart bulbs, wifi-connected switches and plugs contribute to a well lit, well-connected home.

Our lighting solution can be retro fitted into existing homes or installed into new homes. Use your existing lights and wiring circuits.  Dimmable or non-dimmable switches are available.

Control your lights from HomeKit, create automated scenes, or say "Hey Siri, Main room lights 20%"

Keep your switches

Many solutions require new switches or wall plates, and that can become expensive.

Our solution can utilise your existing switches.

The smarts are in here

WiFi wall switches are supplied and must be installed by qualified electricians behind your existing wall plates.
They take up next to no space and can be tucked into the wall or ceiling.

To dim or not to dim?

On/Off switches and Dimmable switches are available.

If your existing bulbs and/or transformers are dimmable you can instruct Siri to dim the lights too!
"Siri, set the Main Room light to 10%"
