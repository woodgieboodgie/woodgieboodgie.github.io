Blinds

Centralised control of every blind in your house

Have your blinds respond when the air conditioning activates or when the house is armed at night to ensure the blinds are closed. Open up a world of possibilities with motorised roller blinds. It will add ease, convenience and comfort, while saving money on your energy bills.

Integrate existing motorised blinds, Many customers want to upgrade their existing blinds without having to replace them and 240 volt or battery solutions cater to that requirement. Most popular brands are Somfy, Acmeda.. however we work with many more.

Keep your rollers

Retro fit your existing roller blinds by fitting motors that can be hard wired or battery operated. If you choose battery, the system will alert when to recharge.

Smarten your venetians or curtains

Ever wanted those old venetian's to open and close automatically? Now it's possible with small motors (or switches if you already have motors) that fit into the head rail and control the tilt rod. Hard wired or battery available.

HomeKit Control

Using HomeKit on your iPhone or iPad or iWatch simply control the level that you would like your blinds to be. Set a scene to open blinds at dawn and close them at dusk.
