Blinds

Centralised control of every light circuit in your house

Open up a world of possibilities with motorised roller blinds. It will add ease, convenience and comfort, while saving money on your energy bills. Many customers want to upgrade their existing blinds without having to replace them and our 240 volt or battery solutions cater to that requirement.

Click on the tiles below for additional information:

Keep your rollers

Retro fit your existing roller blinds by fitting motors that can be hard wired or battery operated. If you choose battery, the system will alert when to recharge.

Smarten your venetians

Ever wanted those old venetians to open and close automatically?
Now it's possible with small motors that fit into the head rail and control the tilt rod.  Hard wired or battery operated motors available.

Got an old remote?

We can install infra red repeaters that send the same signal to your blinds as the old remote control does.

Homekit easy!                   

Using HomeKit on your iPhone or iPad or iWatch simply control the level that you would like your blinds to be.
