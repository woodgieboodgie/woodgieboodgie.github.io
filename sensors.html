Network Sensors

Easily determine if devices are connected to your network

A typical home has plenty of devices connected to the internet, some hard wired others wireless.

There are many reasons why a network might misbehave, so wouldnt it be handy to see at a glance when devices are connected or not without having to log onto the router or call an I.T professional?

Whether it be a networked printer, a phone or watch, a Chromecast or a laptop... anything device that you wish to know whether it is connected to your network or not.

See all devices in a single pane view and optionally set it up to be notified via HomeKit if a device is connected.

Good to be in the know

Devices can be configured to appear in the status of the home and alerts can be easily turned on or off, or set only alert between certain times.
