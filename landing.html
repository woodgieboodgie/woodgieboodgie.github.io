Why House-Mate?

The Home App

Designed by Apple and preinstalled on all their devices, the Home app is simple and easy to use.


Home View

View some of the main details of your home from a dashboard view, such as which accessories are in use, cameras, and favourite accessories.


Accessories View

All of your connected accessories are here, each label can be pressed to open the details view for that device. The icon can be pressed to toggle the device on and off.


Details View

Devices with multiple states, such as dimmable lights, heating/cooling thermostats, security systems, etc. can be precisely controlled from here.
