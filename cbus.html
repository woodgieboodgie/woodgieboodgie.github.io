Smart CBUS Integration

Control CBUS using HomeKit™ and Siri™ from anywhere

House-mate integration solutions connect CBUS controllers to your Wi-Fi network and enable full control via HomeKit™ or Siri™.

Do you have an CBUS system that stands alone?  Why not integrate it into HomeKit and control any element of your CBUS home via your mobile or voice. 

The Clipsal CNI bridges connectivity between your CBUS installation and your local network.  Our service includes all installation, programming and configuration of your Groups and Devices. 

First step

In order for your CBUS to talk to House-mate, a bridge called the Cbus Network Interface needs to be installed by a qualified CBUS electrician. RRP $850, install ~$350.

Keep you CBUS switches

Existing wall switches can be kept and their functionality controlled via voice or from your iPhone, 

Mix CBUS with other tech in your home

Want CBUS to control the thermostat too? Or perhaps the irrigation, or some additonal power points, or the TV?  With this technology you can as CBUS objects are blended with any other devices and presented together within HomeKit.
