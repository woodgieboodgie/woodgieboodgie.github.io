CBUS Integration

Gain control of your CBUS system and make it a whole lot smarter

Do you have an CBUS system that stands alone?

Why not integrate it into HomeKit and control any element of your CBUS home via your mobile or via voice.

Upgrade your home with Smart Voice Controlled Lighting and Switches with access from Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa or Siri.

The Clipsal CNI bridges connectivity between your CBUS installation and your local network.  

Service includes all programming and configuration of Groups/Devices. 

First step

In order for your CBUS to talk to House-mate, a CBUS piece of equipment called the 5500CNI needs to be installed.
This needs to be installed by a qualified CBUS electrician. RRP $850.

Keep you CBUS switches

Existing wall switches can be kept and their functionality controlled via voice or from your iPhone, 

Mix CBUS with other tech in your home

Want CBUS to control the thermostat too? Or perhaps the irrigation, or some additonal power points, or the TV?  With House-mate you can as CBUS objects are blended with any others within HomeKit.
