Smart Garage Door

Control garage doors using HomeKit™ and Siri™ from anywhere

House-mate smart garage solutions connect door controllers to your Wi-Fi network and enable door control using HomeKit™ or Siri™.

Instruct Siri to open or close the smart garage door as you approach. No longer is there a need for the old remote control. Conveniently operate your garage door controller or gates from anywhere using the HomeKit™ app or by voice via Siri™. 

Google and Alexa solutions also available.

Always know the status of your garage door, and safely open and close it from anywhere. A smart garage can alert you if the door is left open unintentionally when nobody is at home.

Place a camera in the garage and securely accept deliveries.
