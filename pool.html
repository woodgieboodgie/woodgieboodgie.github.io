Pool & Spa Automation

Pool and spa control using HomeKit™ and Siri™ from anywhere

House-mate smart pool and spa solutions connect pool controllers to your Wi-Fi network enabling full control via HomeKit™ or Siri™.

Pool and spa automation will simplify your pool maintenance, save money on your energy bills, prevent damage to your pool equipment, and ultimately save you valuable time.  

Control pool and spa water temperature, lights, pumps, filtration, and even chemicals automatically.  You can manage and control your pool settings from wherever you are, 24 hours a day.

Heat up the spa before you come home so it's ready for you to jump straight in!

Convenience

Your pool and spa are all about enhancing your lifestyle so incorporating it into a home automation solution enables you to enjoy your pool within the same app that controls the rest of your home.

Remote control

The solution connects to your pool's equipment via a hub that is accessible via the internet. The app then allows you to control those features from your mobile device. Most popular brands with clients are Aqualink and AstralPool. 

HomeKit interface

Full control delivered to Homekit, meaning you dont have to visit the pool controller to change the temperature or operate the pumps, etc.  "Hey Siri, set the spa heater to 35 degrees"
