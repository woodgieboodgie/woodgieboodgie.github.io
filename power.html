Power Points

Centralised control of any power point in your house

Smart power points are very affordable, can monitor your electricity usage, and in many cases save you money.

Whether it be a coffee machine, a lamp, an electric blanket or a pump... take control of the outlet at the outlet.

Smart power points that allow you to control the power point manually but also harness the ability to control them via HomeKit, or have them participate in automations.

Smart power points

A number of compatible brands produce smart power points. Many can turn a regular power point into a USB charger at the same time.

Schedule the power point to operate at certain times or link them to a scene.

Easy and simple to use

The power point remains completely accessible and can be operated manually.
However, these devices also pack alot of smarts and can be programmed.
Turn a light on when the doorbell detects movement whilst the house is armed.

HomeKit control

Using the rich application designed and supported by Apple, control any smart power point in your home, or have it participate in automations.
